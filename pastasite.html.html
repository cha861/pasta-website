Recipe Of My Favourite Pasta

This is my favourite one.this is completely made in indian style.If you like the recipe try it now

Ingredients:

  1. pasta
  2. veggies
  3. salt
  4. oil
  5. spices
  6. sausages(optional)
  7. water
  8. pepper

Procedure

step 1:

1.Boil the water.

Make sure that you boil your water in high heat for 5 minutes.

step 2:

2.then add the pasta.

Add your pasta in boiling water.and wait for it to boil completely.If you want you can also add few drops of oil into the boiling water to get the non sticky pasta.

step 3:

3.then add the salt.

Add the required amount of salt into the boiling pasta.

step 4:

4.Then add the veggies.

Add the required amount of veggies into the boiling pasta.

step 5:

5.Keep boiled pasta aside

now filter the boiled pasta from boiling water and add it into another bowl and keep it aside.

step 6:

6.Now prepare the masala mix

Take a pan and add few spoons of oil.then add the indian spices into it.Also add salt to it as needed.

step 7:

7.add pepper if needed,based on your taste.

step 8:

8.Mix the pasta and masala

After preparing the pasta's masala ,add the boiled pasta into it.and mix well.

step 9:

9.Add sausages.

You can also add sausages if needed,it is optional.

Thus our favourite pasta recipe is ready to serve.
